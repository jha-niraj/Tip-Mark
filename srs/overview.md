# TipMark — Project Overview

## What is TipMark?

TipMark is a decentralized tip jar and supporter rewards platform built on Solana. It lets fans tip their favorite creators directly in SOL, and in return receive a **permanent on-chain Compressed NFT badge** that proves they supported that creator — before they blew up.

Think: *Buy Me a Coffee* but decentralized, zero-fee, and with permanent proof-of-support badges.

## The Problem

Existing tip/donation tools:
- **Buy Me a Coffee / Ko-fi** — centralized, 5% fees, no crypto, no permanent record
- **Basic Solana tip jars** — just send SOL, tipper gets nothing back, no social proof
- **TipLink** — focuses on sending SOL as links, not creator-fan relationships

**What's missing**: Tippers have no permanent, verifiable record of their support. Creators have no way to reward early supporters. There's no social layer on top of tipping.

## The Solution: Tip-to-Earn Supporter Badges

When a fan tips a creator on TipMark:
1. SOL is transferred directly to the creator (no middleman, no fees)
2. A **Compressed NFT badge** is minted instantly to the tipper's wallet
3. The badge is **soul-bound** (non-transferable) — it's proof you were there
4. Badge tier is based on tip amount (Bronze / Silver / Gold / Diamond)
5. Creators can **token-gate** content, Discord, or exclusive perks for badge holders

## Target Users

- **Creators**: YouTubers, streamers, podcasters, open-source devs, writers
- **Fans**: Anyone who wants to support creators and get permanent on-chain proof
